Privacy Policy for Bucket

Last Updated: April 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bucket. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use our service.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Bucket is designed as a personal finance tracking tool.

  • User-Entered Data: All financial records, budget categories, and transaction details you enter are stored locally on your device or within your private storage.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal financial data to third parties.
  • Permissions: The app may request specific permissions (such as SMS, Notifications, or Storage) solely to facilitate core functionality like automated expense tracking or data export. This processing happens locally on the device.

3. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Since Bucket operates primarily as a local-first application:

  • Your data is as secure as your mobile device.
  • We recommend using device-level security (PIN, biometrics) to prevent unauthorized access to the app.

4. Third-Party Services

If you choose to integrate third-party services (such as Google Drive for backups), their use of your information will be governed by their respective privacy policies. Bucket only accesses these services to perform the tasks you explicitly authorize.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
